Welcome! This tool generates AI-powered equity research briefs — similar to what analysts at investment banks produce.

How to use: Type any stock ticker (like AAPL for Apple, or RELIANCE for Reliance Industries) and click Generate.

Single vs Compare: Use Single Stock for a deep-dive. Use Compare Stocks to analyze two companies side by side.

Quick glossary:
• P/E Ratio — Price-to-Earnings. How much investors pay per dollar of earnings.
• Market Cap — Total value of all shares.
• ROE — Return on Equity. How efficiently the company uses shareholder money.
• Bull Case — Reasons to buy. Bear Case — Reasons to avoid.
• Outlook — Overall verdict: Bullish, Neutral, or Bearish.
