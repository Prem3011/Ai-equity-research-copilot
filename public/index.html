📊 Financial terms explained

• P/E Ratio — Price-to-Earnings. How much investors pay per dollar of profit. Lower often means cheaper.
• Market Cap — Total value of all company shares. Shows how big the company is.
• ROE — Return on Equity. How well the company turns shareholder money into profit. Higher is better.
• Debt/Equity — How much debt vs shareholder money. Lower is generally safer.
• Bull Case — Reasons the stock could go UP. Bear Case — Reasons it could go DOWN.
• Outlook — The AI's overall verdict: 🟢 Bullish (positive), 🟡 Neutral, or 🔴 Bearish (negative).
